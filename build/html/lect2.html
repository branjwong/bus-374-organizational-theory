Why are there so many different types of organizations?¶

  • “managers were thought of as all capable individual”

  • different species of organizations

  • survival of the fittest

    • by fit, we mean suiting the current set of needs, given infrastructure
    • not best

  • punishment for deviance: you are not this thing if you deviate in this way

    • that’s how you know this thing is in fact this thing
